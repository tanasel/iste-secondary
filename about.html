About this prototype

This site is an independent prototype created by Alexandru Tănăsel, a Computer Science and Digital Design teacher at the International School of The Hague, to show a practical, secondary-focused way of putting the ISTE Standards into classroom practice. It is a proposal and a conversation-starter — not official school policy, and not affiliated with or endorsed by ISTE.

Purpose

Why this exists

Good standards fail without good implementation and coaching; this turns the framework into something a busy secondary teacher can actually use on Monday.
